Everland theme park

Everland is South Korea's largest theme park, located in Yongin, just outside of Seoul. It offers a mix of thrilling rides, like the famous T-Express wooden roller coaster, along with family-friendly attractions, seasonal festivals, a zoo, and a beautiful flower garden. Operated by Samsung C&T, Everland combines entertainment, nature, and culture, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists.
